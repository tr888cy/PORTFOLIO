Path of Commons

Academic work, spring 2018

Tutor: Ning SHAO, Qiguo LI

Individual project

The students in Wuhan University are now yearning for new possibilities of the 3rd space that can effectively answer to the site limitation where there is not much space for semi-formal communication. Path of Commons, a campus complex at the junction of living zone and education zone, is an attempt to create a new active outdoor space where people can comfortably enjoy it at anytime of the year.

Path of Commons proposes a vertical open-air public space folding upward as a backbone of the building. It starts with the ground which is a landscape steps which integrated with platforms, seatings, planting and exhibition boards, connecting the boundary of the site with height difference. “The Ground’ vertically opens up through large voids on the upper floors, connecting to a large public open-air area occupying nearly thirty percent of upper floor plates.
